Mission

What we do every day.

ICF Cambodia walks alongside vulnerable children, youth, and families through holistic church, social, educational, medical, and leadership programs — strengthening individuals, families, and communities for long-term flourishing.

Our Values

Excellent

We give our best in every small thing — because the people we serve deserve our best work.

Relevant

We meet people where they are — in their language, their culture, and their reality.

Excited

Joy and energy are core to who we are — we bring them to the work and to each other.

Authentic

Real, honest, transparent. We don't perform — we live what we believe.

Hospitable

Every person — staff, visitor, child, family — is welcomed home.

The New ICF Campus

See full master plan →

Let's build the future

2025 was a year of significant progress. With our partnership with PETRAM Design International, the master plan and updated 3D visualizations give a clear picture of the new campus, stepping forward from concept design into detailed design in 2026. By the end of 2025, all administrative documentation was finalized and the building permit application was officially submitted.
