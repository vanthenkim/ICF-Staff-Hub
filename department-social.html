Social

Walking alongside vulnerable families through community outreach, social work, mental health support, and daycare — rooted in compassion and dignity.

What We Do

Community Outreach
Connecting with vulnerable families and communities in Phnom Penh through relational outreach, needs assessment, and holistic support.
Social Work Services
Professional social workers providing case management, referrals, and direct support to individuals and families in need.
Mental Health Support
Providing professional mental health care through our mental health coordinator — supporting both community members and ICF families.
Daycare Programme
Full-day childcare for young children of ICF families and community members — providing a safe, nurturing environment.
Case Administration
Managing referrals, documentation, and follow-up across all social programs — ensuring continuity of care for every family we serve.
