Catering

Daily staff meals, event catering, and kitchen operations — keeping the ICF Cambodia campus fed and fuelled.

What We Do

Daily Staff Meals
Preparing and serving lunch for ICF Cambodia staff each working day, including Daycare children's meals.
Event Catering
Providing food and refreshments for internal events, trainings, donor visits, and special occasions across campus.
Kitchen Hygiene & Safety
Maintaining a clean, safe, and compliant kitchen environment in line with ICF Kitchen Policy and food safety standards.
