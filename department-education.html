Education

Empowering children and youth through after-school programmes, creative arts, English, sports, and scholarships — opening doors to a better future.

Lead: Parigna Souem Team: 14 staff Contact: parigna.souem@icf-cambodia.com

What We Do

After School Programme
Structured after-school activities providing academic support, creative learning, and personal development for children from disadvantaged backgrounds.
Creative Arts
Art, craft, and creative expression classes that build confidence and communication skills while nurturing each child's unique talents.
English Classes
English language instruction to improve communication skills and expand opportunities for ICF-connected youth.
Sports & Physical Education
PE and sports programming that promotes health, teamwork, and discipline — giving children positive outlets for energy and development.
Scholarship Programme
Supporting academically gifted students from low-income families with school and university scholarships — investing in Cambodia's next generation.
